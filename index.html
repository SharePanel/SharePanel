Why Use This Service?

In the digital age, the success of any organization or business is heavily dependent on its online presence. At SharePanel, we offer top-down management and hosting software that delivers the same high-quality services typically offered at premium prices. Our platform ensures that your business can maintain a strong and effective network presence without breaking the bank.

With SharePanel, you gain access to a comprehensive suite of tools designed to streamline your operations, improve efficiency, and enhance your digital footprint. Our software provides robust hosting solutions, intuitive management features, and seamless integration capabilities, all tailored to meet the unique needs of your organization.

Our Service Includes

  • Secure Authentication One of the most critical elements in software development is the ability to securely store and preserve user credentials. At SharePanel, we prioritize the security of user information and provide robust measures to ensure it remains protected. In case of forgotten passwords, we offer secure email password reset codes to help users regain access to their accounts safely and efficiently.
  • Team-Permissions SharePanel offers varying levels of permissions to users within an organization, allowing for precise control over who can edit, view, and access different parts of the system. These permissions are designed to manage everything from general viewing to detailed editing capabilities. Additionally, SharePanel includes advanced options for restricting user access to highly specific and purposeful locations, ensuring that sensitive information and critical functions are only accessible to authorized personnel.
  • Direct Messages Our direct messaging feature enables users to easily chat within the organization, making it simple to request help, assistance, or any other information.
  • Hosting Control the timing of your webpage updates with SharePanel! Host HTML, CSS, and JS files for free, with options to enable PHP upon request in specific cases. Pricing is determined monthly based on factors such as size, bandwidth, and number of files hosted.
  • Admin Panel Use the admin-dashboard to control user permissions, add new users, remove old users, and edit existing users.

Using SharePanel is a very easy way to get a site out there and start working on it as a team. There are more resources for setting up your first app etc.
